| <p>**其他重要的安全注意事项**</p><p>**射频干扰**</p><p>请注意观察禁止或限制使用电子设备的标志和告示。来自此产品的辐射有可能对其他电子设备造成影响，导致它们出现故障。例如：心脏起搏器附近、医疗机构及医疗电子设备附近、飞机机舱内。</p><p>**医疗设备干扰**</p><p>此产品包含的磁体以及部分组件会发射电磁场。这些磁体和电磁场可能干扰医疗设备，例如人工耳蜗、助听器、心脏起搏器等其他医疗设备。请使相关医疗设备与此产品保持安全距离。如怀疑产品正在干扰您的医疗设备，请立即停止使用并咨询医师。</p><p>**健康问题**</p><p>当不断重复同一动作时（例如打字、玩游戏），您的手、臂、腕、肩、颈或其他身体部位可能会感到不适。如果您感觉到不适，请停止使用此产品，适当休息，如有需要可咨询医师。</p><p>**后果严重的活动**</p><p>此产品不适合用于可能因设备故障而导致死亡、人身伤害或重大环境破坏的活动。</p><p>**窒息危险**</p><p>此产品含有细小部件，某些部件可能会造成孩童窒息或其它伤害。请将此产品放在孩童接触不到的地方。</p><p>**液体入侵** </p><p>此产品是一种内含大量电路和电容的电子产品，请注意防止水或其它液体的浸入，因为这可能会导致产品损坏或其他安全问题。</p><p>**拆解产品**</p><p>对此产品进行任何未经授权的维修、改装、拆解或撕毁产品标贴都将使产品失去保修。</p><p>**静电电击**</p><p>在空气比较干燥的区域使用（金属外壳版本）此产品时，容易聚集静电，您接触产品外壳时可能会遭受产品的静电放电。请避免在极其干燥的环境中使用此产品。或者在使用此产品前触摸已接地的未上漆的金属物体。</p><p>**设备的废弃处理**</p><p>不得将具有此符号的设备混入生活垃圾一同废弃处理。根据法律规定，在您的经销商处或市政收集点废弃处理该设备。</p><p>**内置电池安全（此处仅适用于含电池版本的产品）**</p><p>产品内置不可拆卸电池。请勿自行拆卸、更换或刺穿电池。任何不当操作可能导致电池损坏、过热、泄漏、起火或爆炸，并存在安全风险。</p><p>请仅使用产品随附的原装数据线，并通过电脑的 USB 端口为设备充电。使用非原装线缆、第三方充电器或不符合规格的电源，可能导致充电异常、电池性能下降或产生安全隐患。</p><p>请在通风良好、环境温度适宜的条件下为产品充电。避免将产品放置在床、沙发等柔软表面，或置于密闭容器中，以防过热。</p><p>避免将产品长时间暴露于极端温度环境中（包括但不限于暴晒的车辆内部、靠近火源的区域或极端寒冷的户外环境），否则可能影响电池性能、缩短使用寿命，甚至引发安全问题。</p><p>产品废弃时，请勿作为生活垃圾处理。请遵循当地法律法规的要求，将产品交由指定的电子废弃物回收机构处理。</p> | <p>**关于产品的防泼溅说明**</p><p>本产品采用防泼溅设计，可在规定测试条件下，降低因日常少量液体意外泼溅导致产品损坏的风险。</p><p> </p><p>请注意，本产品并非防水产品，防泼溅能力并非永久有效，可能因产品老化、跌落或碰撞、拆卸维修、日常磨损或实际使用环境等因素而下降。同时，相关测试均在受控实验室条件下进行，不代表产品适用于所有实际使用场景，例如高温热水、海水、泳池、桑拿、高压水流、化学液体、饮料等环境。</p><p> </p><p>为确保产品正常使用，请避免将产品浸泡于液体中，或在潮湿、积水及其他超出产品设计条件的环境下使用。因液体进入产品内部所造成的损坏，不属于产品保修范围。 请妥善使用产品，并定期检查产品外观是否存在可能影响产品防护性能的损伤。</p><p> </p><p>**如键盘不慎接触液体，请立即按以下步骤处理：**</p><p>1. 立即断开电源。不带内置电池的产品请立即拔下 USB 数据线；带内置电池的产品请立即关闭电源。 </p><p>2. 使用干燥、柔软的布擦拭键盘表面的液体，并将键盘放置于通风、干燥的环境中自然晾干。 </p><p>3. 在确认键盘内部完全干燥之前，请勿重新通电或连接设备，以免造成短路或进一步损坏。 </p><p>4. 如液体进入产品内部，或接触咖啡、可乐、果汁等含糖、含盐或具有腐蚀性的液体，建议尽快联系 IQUNIX 售后服务进行检测和处理。</p> |

| <p>**Other Important Safety Information**</p><p>**Radio Frequency Interference**</p><p>Please observe all signs and notices that prohibit or restrict the use of electronic devices.</p><p>This product may emit radiation that can interfere with nearby electronic equipment, potentially causing malfunctions. Examples include areas near pacemakers, medical facilities, medical electronic devices, and aircraft cabins.</p><p>**Medical Device Interference**</p><p>This product contains magnets and components that emit electromagnetic fields. These may interfere with medical devices such as pacemakers, cochlear implants, hearing aids, and other medical equipment. Maintain a safe distance between this product and any such devices. If you suspect interference, stop using the product immediately and consult your physician.</p><p>**Health Concerns**</p><p>Repetitive activities, such as typing or gaming, may cause discomfort in your hands, wrists, arms, shoulders, neck, or other parts of the body. If you experience discomfort, stop using the product and rest. Consult a physician if symptoms persist.</p><p>**High-Risk Activities**</p><p>This product is not intended for use in situations where a malfunction could result in death, personal injury, or severe environmental damage.</p><p>**Choking Hazard**</p><p>The product contains small parts that may pose a choking hazard or cause other injuries to children. Please keep the product out of reach of children.</p><p>**Liquid Ingress**</p><p>This electronic device contains internal circuits and capacitors. Avoid exposure to water or other liquids, as this may result in product damage or pose safety risks.</p><p>**Product Disassembly**</p><p>Any unauthorized repairs, modifications, disassembly, or removal of product labels will void the warranty.</p><p>**Static Electric Shock**</p><p>When using the product (metal shell version) in relatively dry environments, static electricity can build up. You may experience an electrostatic discharge when touching the product casing. To minimize this risk, avoid using the product in extremely dry environments. Alternatively, touch a grounded, unpainted metal object before handling the product.</p><p>**Equipment Disposal**</p><p>Devices bearing this symbol must not be disposed of with household waste.  Follow local regulations for disposal, either through your dealer or an authorized municipal collection point.</p><p>**Built-in Battery Safety (For Battery-Included Versions Only)**</p><p>Only use the original data cable supplied with the product and charge the device via a computer USB port. The use of non-original cables, third-party chargers, or incompatible power sources may cause abnormal charging, battery degradation, or safety risks.</p><p>This product contains a non-removable built-in battery. Do not attempt to disassemble, replace, or puncture the battery. Improper handling may result in battery damage, overheating, leakage, fire, or explosion, and may pose serious safety hazards.</p><p>Charge the device in a well-ventilated environment with an appropriate ambient temperature. Do not place it on soft surfaces (such as beds or sofas),  or in enclosed spaces to prevent overheating.</p><p>Avoid prolonged exposure to extreme temperatures (including but not limited to sun-exposed vehicles, areas near heat sources, or extremely cold environments).Such conditions may negatively affect battery performance, reduce service life, and compromise safety.</p><p>Dispose of the product in accordance with local regulations. Do not discard it with household waste; recycle through designated electronic waste collection facilities.</p> | <p>**About Splash Resistance**</p><p>This product is designed with splash-resistant protection to help reduce the risk of damage caused by accidental exposure to small amounts of liquid under specified test conditions.</p><p>Please note that this product is not waterproof. Splash resistance is not permanent and may diminish over time due to normal wear, aging, drops or impacts, disassembly or repair, or other environmental and usage conditions. Splash-resistance testing is performed under controlled laboratory conditions and does not represent all real-world situations, including exposure to hot water, seawater, swimming pools, saunas, high-pressure water, chemicals, beverages, or similar environments.</p><p>To help ensure normal operation, do not immerse the product in liquid or use it in environments that exceed its designed operating conditions, such as wet or flooded areas. Damage caused by liquid entering the product is not covered under the product warranty. Handle the product with care and periodically inspect it for any signs of damage that may affect its splash-resistant performance.</p><p /><p>**If your keyboard comes into contact with liquid, follow these steps immediately:**</p><p>1. Disconnect the power immediately. For products without a built-in battery, unplug the USB cable immediately. For products with a built-in battery, turn off the power immediately.</p><p>2. Wipe away any liquid from the surface of the keyboard using a soft, dry cloth, then place the keyboard in a well-ventilated, dry area and allow it to air dry naturally. </p><p>3. Do not reconnect the keyboard to a device or restore power until you are certain that the inside of the keyboard is completely dry, as doing so may cause a short circuit or further damage. </p><p>4. If liquid has entered the product, or if the product has been exposed to coffee, soft drinks, juice, or other liquids containing sugar, salt, or corrosive substances, contact IQUNIX Customer Support as soon as possible for inspection and assistance.</p> |
